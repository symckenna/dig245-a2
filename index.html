Viewing HTML in Chrome on an Apple Macbook

"Behind the Core of the Apple"

  1. Go to the website of your choice (for this example, we will be visiting Airbnb)
  2. Click the "View" tab at the top of your computer screen.
  3. Scroll down to the "Developer" selection, then scroll over to the "View Source" selection.
  4. Click the "View Source" to see the HTML code for Airbnb.
  5. To delete seeing the "View Source" HTML code, just delete the "view-source:" from the URL in Chrome and refresh the page.
